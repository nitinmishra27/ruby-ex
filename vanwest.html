To everyone in our District family!

We have just concluded our first two major events of our District Calendar for this season. Men's retreat was a great time of fellowship, ministry and renewal as was the Ladies retreat just  last week. What a wonderful time all the ladies had and again it was a timely event that ministered to the ladies coupled with the fellowship and fun. It was also a great success.
 
Now we are approaching another premier event for our District. District camp meeting which will begin July 16 and run through the 20th. We look forward to gathering from all across our District to join in worship and fellowship once again at Dogwood Valley Camp, Hope BC.
 
This year we will be blessed by the ministry of Rev. Randy Ensey our South Central UPCI Executive Presbyter and Pastor of "Living Way Church" of Conroe TX. He will be helping us with our business and will be preaching the first night of our camp meeting.  



Rev. Mark Morgan, District Superintendent of the Western District and pastor of "Abounding Grace" in the city of San Fransisco, CA will be ministering in the evening service Wednesday through Friday. you will be greatly blessed by Bro. Morgans ministry!




The Youth Dept. will be having youth services with Rev. Kaleb Saucer.

BC NAM will have a special day on Wednesday and Will have an emphasis in the Wednesday evening service. 

BC Children's Ministries will be running the children program each day Tuesday through Friday at 10:00 am

In all it will be again a great time of God's people coming together. We hope you are making plans to be in attendance this year. It truly will be a great time together and in the presence of the Lord.
